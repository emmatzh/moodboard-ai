# Moodly 🎨🌿

> Your aesthetic. Your mood. Your world.

Moodly is a two-in-one creative wellness web app built in 24 hours at Code Camp — Digital Women Hackathon. It combines AI-curated moodboard generation with a daily mental health check-in tool, all in one seamless split-screen experience.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎨 Moodboard Generator
- Each board includes a color palette, matching images, a quote, and typography
- 9 curated vibes — Calm, Happy, Stressed, Lonely, Tired, Sad, Angry, Excited, Hopeful
- Upload your own photo to personalize your board
- Download your moodboard as a high-resolution PNG

### 🌿 Daily Mental Health Check-in
- Pick how you're feeling with an emoji
- The app's color theme reacts instantly to your mood
- Write about your day and get a personalized affirmation
- Curated Spotify playlist matched to your mood
- Daily streak tracker — shows how many days in a row you've shown up for yourself

### 🔗 Connected Experience
- Selecting an emoji automatically generates a matching moodboard on the right
- Mood → Affirmation → Playlist → Moodboard — all in one flow

## 🎯 How It Works

1. User selects an emoji reflecting their current mood
2. The left panel instantly reacts — color theme changes to match the mood
3. A matching moodboard generates on the right simultaneously
4. User writes about their day and clicks Get My Affirmation
5. A personalized affirmation and Spotify playlist appear
6. User can also manually explore vibes using the tag picker or text input
7. Download button saves the moodboard as a PNG
